Train as much or little as you’d like through the holidays. If anything and as always, when in doubt - squat!

Day 1

Strength: 

1) Back squat: 65%x 8 reps, 75%x 6 reps, 85%x 4 reps, 90%x 4 reps.

*Rest 10 minutes, then:

2) Front squat: 70%x 5 reps, 80%x 4 reps, 85%x 3 reps, 90%x 3 reps.

Work Capacity: In 10 minutes, perform:

-“Isabel”: 30x snatch (135#/95#)

-Max meters on the rower

Core: 3 rounds:

-5x GHD back extensions with 2 second hold at the top (barbell)

-rest 90 seconds

-10x strict toes to bar

-rest as needed

Day 2

Strength: 

1) Pause snatch balance: 10 rounds – Every 60 seconds perform:

-1x snatch balance with a 3 second pause in the receiving/bottom position. Work up to a max for the day.

*Start with a snatch balance, no dip and when you fail, re-rack the weight and continue climbing up the ladder with a snatch balance.

2) Snatch: 10 rounds – Every 90 seconds, perform:

-1x squat snatch (Work up to a max for the day).

Work Capacity: Complete the following for time:

-15x muscle up

-500m row

-10x muscle up

-500m row

-5x muscle up

*Any time you break the muscle up, immediately perform 3x front squats (155#/105#)

Day 3

Strength:

1) Back squat: 65%x 6, 75%x 6, 80%x6x2 sets

*Rest 10 minutes, then

2) Front squat: 60%x 5, 65%x 5, 70%x5x2 sets

Work Capacity:

Complete the following for time:

-50x unbroken double unders
-40x unbroken thruster (75#/55#)
-30x unbroken GHD sit ups
-20x unbroken burpee pull up
-10x unbroken hang squat clean (135#/95#)
*rest 2 minutes, then for max calories

-Tabata air dyne/assault bike

Tabata = 8 rounds: 20 seconds max effort, 10 seconds rest.

Core:

3 rounds:

-5x front rack Sotts press (AHAP)
-90 seconds rest

-5x skin the cats on barbell
-rest as needed

Day 4

Strength:

1: Block cleans: 10 rounds- Every 2 minutes, compete:

-3x squat clean from 1″ below the knee (work up to a max triple for the day)

2: 10 rounds (20 minutes)- EMOM, perform:

-Odd minute = 1x split jerk behind the neck (work up to a max)

-Even minute = 2x rope climb, legless if possible.

Work Capacity:

Complete the following for time:

-“Karen” 150x wallball (20#/14#)

*any time you break, immediately perform a 200m run.

Core:

-Establish a 1 RM good morning

Day 5 (Optional):

1) Conditioning/work capacity: In 60 minutes, complete the following:

-Row 10k

In the remaining time, perform AMRAP of:

-10m walking lunge

-10x push up

-10m walking lunge

-30 second plank
